About our company

Kurt&Kurt LLC was established in 2008 to promote medical equipment in the market. Our company carries out import-export and exclusive representative activities providing turnkey solutions for hospitals and after-sales technical service to its customers all over the country. After years of experience in the market, Kurt&Kurt has achieved a leading position in the field of radiology and endoscopy. This was mainly achieved by ensuring maximum customer satisfaction.
